1. The Current Situation

So what does New Zealand do to increase our prosperity? We should look at what we are currently doing and where we can improve.
This visualisation attempts to show this, by presenting us with a graph of what industries produce what exports, and where those exports are sold.

If you select manufacturing you will notice that we export a very wide array of items to many different countries. These areas of export are largely very small with room for expansion.

You can also click on agriculture industry, or the dairy produce export to see how important it is to our economy.

2. How it Works

Select either an industry, an export or a country to find out what we are making, and where we are selling it.
The size of the links represent the amount of money that flows from industries to export to other countries.
